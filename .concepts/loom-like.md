### Loom-like interfaces

Many more interfaces are similar to a [[loom.md|loom]] or aspirationally similar to one, than we give credit for.

- Roam Research is like a loom - it encourages people to lay out their thoughts as outlines and then recombine them. Daily notes allow re-weaving of narratives.
- Codex is like a loom - you can queue up a bunch of different questions about a task at once. Context windows don't stop you from having multiple unrelated conversations in the same interface, although branching support is near nonexistent.[^1]
- Creating a list of projects and or interests sending it to someone exposes an interface that's like a loom - because they can request that any of those topics get expanded, but there's also a clear and legible interface to the branches.

_From a conversation with [[Ivan Vendrov]] in November 2025._

[^1]: At the time of writing Claude Code doesn't support queueing messages.
