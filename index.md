### Human-AI Interaction Lab Notes

I'm making [Braid](https://braid.md), a multiplayer loom, and [Input](https://input.md), a frontend for GitHub that lets you use Git repos as wikis and knowledge bases.

Both are pre-release software and have rough edges, but you're welcome to login and play around.

Braid changes rapidly and your data may be lost at any time.

Input is designed to stick around, but will move to a different domain soon, since Twitter now blocks .md domains from being recognized as links.
