### A quick detour

Related to item one, I started a quick exploration on Monday, of Kyle Mathews' [Electric Monks](https://github.com/KyleAMathews/hegelian-dialectic-skill) skill.

His skill takes a question or tension, and launches a fleet of subagents to argue for different positions related to the question. Then another subagent synthesizes them, tests them, and starts a new round of dialectic.

I finished a full dialectic yesterday, with lots of manual intervention along the way. It was the closest thing to an model looming a human (snav: "hoom") that I have used so far.
